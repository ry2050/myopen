2. Data We May Collect

2.1 Data You Provide

  • We do not collect or upload any content you create in the App. All data stays on your device.

  • If you contact us by email, we receive only the information you voluntarily provide.

2.2 Automatically Collected Technical Data (via RevenueCat)

To process and validate subscriptions, the App uses RevenueCat, which may collect:

  • Anonymous device identifiers (e.g., App User ID)
  • App Store / Google Play transaction history and receipt information.

Aside from subscription-related processing, the App:

  • does not collect analytics data
  • does not collect advertising identifiers
  • does not track users and device identifiers for marketing purposes

2.3 Permission-Based Data

The current version of the App does not request any system permissions (camera, photos, microphone, notifications, location, etc.).

If future versions introduce features requiring permissions, users will be clearly notified and this Policy will be updated.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Although the App does not collect personal data, subscription-related processing by RevenueCat relies on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract: validating purchases and providing paid features
  • Legitimate interest: responding to user support emails

4. How We Use Your Data

Because the App does not collect personal data, we do not use personal data for analytics, marketing, profiling, or any other purpose. Subscription-related data processed by RevenueCat is used solely to:
